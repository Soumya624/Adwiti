Our Mission To make presentations more interesting and easier to understand

Throughout the world, ideas are valued the most and considered to reflect the person. They can make or break your day. Therefore, it is imperative that the idea reaches the audience in the exact manner you intend, if not in a way that is unique, appealing and attention worthy. We at Adwiti are on a mission to ease the presentation process through an interactive video presentation technique so that the ideas can be easily conveyed to everyone.

How we started

We are a group of IITians who are passionate about solving problems using our technological focus and capabilities. It begins with our founders' thirst for a better world, which fuelled the ideas and innovation that we have created. The product that is created has the potential to redefine our world in terms of conveying concepts, so founders claim it is worth taking the risk.
